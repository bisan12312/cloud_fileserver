FAQ

How does DepthNote handle my privacy?

Your questions and notes stay private. Review our Privacy Policy to learn how data is protected.

How do subscriptions work?

Thinking Mode subscriptions are managed through the Microsoft Store or our payment provider. Manage or cancel anytime from your Microsoft account.

Can I use DepthNote offline?

You can view saved notes offline. Asking new questions requires a connection so our AI can reason through your problem.

What about refunds?

Refunds follow the Microsoft Store refund policy. Check their help center for eligibility and timing.
